### Financial Management
- Create and track RA Bills (Architect Certificates)
- Process payments with allocations
- Track secured advances and recovery
- Generate financial reports and dashboards

### Project Management
- Create and manage construction projects
- Track project timelines and milestones
- Monitor project financials
- Manage stakeholders

### Material Management
- Track material inventory
- Record material receipts and issues
- Manage stock levels
- Create requisitions

### Labour Management
- Record daily labour attendance
- Create labour bills and payslips
- Track labour productivity
- Manage labour contractors

### Document Management
- Upload and store project documents
- Version control for documents
- Download and retrieve documents
- Audit trail for all changes
