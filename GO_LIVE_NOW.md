## 📞 **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### Login not working?
1. Check email is exact: `demo-admin@example.com`
2. Check password has `!` at end: `DemoPass123!`
3. Clear browser cache & try again

### Pages loading slowly?
1. Check health: `/health/ready`
2. Wait 30 seconds
3. Hard refresh: `Ctrl+F5`

### Data not saving?
1. Check browser console for errors: `F12`
2. Verify backend is online: `/health`
3. Check network tab for failed requests
